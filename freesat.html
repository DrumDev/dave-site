Freesat Installations Glasgow

Freesat gives you over 150 Digital TV And Radio channels, all for FREE. This includes high definition (HD) programmes, from the BBC + ITV, Digital texts and interactive options.

The Freesat service is received via a satellite. To receive the Standard Definition (SD) Freesat service all you need is a satellite dish and Freesat Digital box, or satellite dish with a Freesat TV. To receive Freesat high definition (HD) you will need a HD ready TV,and a HD freesat Digital box. Just like Freeview Freesat is subscription FREE with no tie-in's.

We can supply and fit your Freesat system anywhere.
